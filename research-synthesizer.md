## SOURCE EVALUATION

### Credibility Checklist
```
AUTHORITY
□ Who is the author?
□ What are their credentials?
□ What institution are they affiliated with?
□ Are they recognized in the field?

ACCURACY
□ Is the information verifiable?
□ Are claims supported by evidence?
□ Is methodology sound?
□ Have errors been identified?

OBJECTIVITY
□ Is the purpose clear?
□ Is there bias or agenda?
□ Are multiple perspectives included?
□ Is language neutral?

CURRENCY
□ When was it published?
□ Is the information still relevant?
□ Have there been updates?
□ Is the field fast-moving?

COVERAGE
□ Is the topic covered thoroughly?
□ What is the intended audience?
□ Are limitations acknowledged?
□ Is it primary or secondary source?
```

## LITERATURE REVIEW TEMPLATES

### Systematic Review Format
```
1. INTRODUCTION
   - Background and rationale
   - Research question
   - Objectives

2. METHODS
   - Search strategy
   - Inclusion/exclusion criteria
   - Data extraction process
   - Quality assessment

3. RESULTS
   - Search results summary
   - Study characteristics
   - Quality assessment results
   - Main findings

4. DISCUSSION
   - Summary of evidence
   - Limitations
   - Comparison with other reviews

5. CONCLUSION
   - Key findings
   - Implications
   - Future research
```

### Narrative Review Format
```
1. INTRODUCTION
   - Topic overview
   - Why this matters
   - Scope of review

2. HISTORICAL CONTEXT
   - How understanding evolved
   - Key milestones

3. CURRENT STATE OF KNOWLEDGE
   - Major theories/models
   - Recent findings
   - Active debates

4. EMERGING TRENDS
   - New directions
   - Innovations
   - Future outlook

5. CONCLUSIONS
   - Key takeaways
   - Remaining questions
   - Recommendations
```

pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx

Publisher, Day Month Year, URL.
