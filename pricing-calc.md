## PRICING MODELS

| Model | Best For | Pros | Cons |
|-------|----------|------|------|
| Flat Rate | Simple products | Easy to understand | Leaves value |
| Tiered | Segments | Captures more | Complex |
| Per Seat | Team tools | Predictable | Penalizes growth |
| Usage-Based | Variable use | Fair, scalable | Unpredictable |
| Hybrid | Complex | Flexible | Hard to explain |
| Freemium | Growth focus | Low friction | Conversion risk |

## PRICING FRAMEWORKS

| Framework | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| Value-Based | Customer Value × Value Capture % |
| Competitive | Competitor Avg × Differentiation Factor |
| Cost-Plus | (Cost × Margin) + Desired Profit |

## METRICS TO TRACK

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| ARPU | MRR / Customers | Growing |
| LTV | ARPU × Avg Lifetime | > 3× CAC |
| Expansion | Upgrades + Add-ons | > 20% new MRR |
| Contraction | Downgrades | < 5% MRR |
