## GDPR REQUIREMENTS

| Requirement | Section | Must Include |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| Lawful basis | Legal Basis | Why you collect |
| Data types | Information Collected | What you collect |
| Rights | Your Rights | User rights list |
| Retention | Data Retention | How long kept |
| Transfers | International | Cross-border data |
| Contact | Contact Us | DPO details |
