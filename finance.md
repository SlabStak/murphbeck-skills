## KEY METRICS

| Metric | Definition | Healthy Range |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| LTV:CAC | Lifetime value / acquisition cost | >3:1 |
| Gross Margin | Revenue - COGS / Revenue | >60% SaaS |
| Burn Multiple | Burn / Net New ARR | <2x |
| Rule of 40 | Growth % + Profit % | >40% |
| Payback | Months to recover CAC | <12 months |
