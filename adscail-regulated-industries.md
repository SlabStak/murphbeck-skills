## REGULATED INDUSTRIES

| Industry | Regulator | Key Requirements |
|----------|-----------|------------------|
| Pharma | FDA | Fair balance, ISI, PI link |
| Finance | SEC/FINRA | Risk disclosure, no guarantees |
| Healthcare | HIPAA/CMS | PHI protection, Medicare rules |
| Insurance | State AGs | Rate claims, coverage accuracy |
| Supplements | FTC | FDA disclaimer, no claims |

## CLAIM TYPES

| Type | Allowed | Requires |
|------|---------|----------|
| Substantiated | Yes | Clinical evidence |
| Qualified | Yes | Appropriate qualifier |
| Comparative | Conditional | Substantiation |
| Testimonial | Conditional | Disclosure |
| Guarantee | No | Never allowed |

## DISCLAIMER LEVELS

| Severity | Content | Placement |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Critical | Full ISI/risks | Prominent |
| Required | Standard disclaimer | Visible |
| Platform | Character-limited | Ad copy |
| Fine print | Legal details | Landing page |
