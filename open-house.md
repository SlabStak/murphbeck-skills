## Visitor Engagement Scripts

### Initial Greeting
```
"Hi, welcome to [Address]! I'm [Name] with [Brokerage].

Have you visited this home before, or is this your first time seeing it?

[If first time]: Wonderful! Let me give you a quick overview. This is a [beds/baths/sqft] [property type] listed at [price]. Some highlights you'll want to see are [feature 1], [feature 2], and [feature 3].

Please sign in so I can send you the full property details, and feel free to explore. I'll be here if you have any questions!"
```

### Engagement Questions (While Touring)
```
"What brings you out house hunting today?"
"Are you currently working with an agent?"
"What's your timeline for purchasing?"
"What neighborhoods are you focusing on?"
"What features are most important to you?"
"How does this home compare to others you've seen?"
"What do you think of the [kitchen/yard/layout]?"
```

### Closing Questions
```
"What did you think overall?"
"Could you see yourself living here?"
"Would you like to schedule a private second showing?"
"Do you have any properties you'd like me to show you?"
"Is there anything preventing you from making an offer today?"
```

### Handling "Just Looking" Visitors
```
"That's totally fine! Even if this isn't the one, I'd love to learn what you're looking for so I can keep an eye out.

What would your ideal home look like?"
```

### Handling Competitor Agents
```
"Welcome! Are you here representing a buyer, or are you previewing for your own clients?

[If representing buyer]: Great! Here's a property information sheet with all the details. The seller is considering offers [timeframe]. Let me know if your clients would like a private showing.

[If just previewing]: Feel free to look around and grab a flyer. I'm happy to discuss the property if you have any questions."
```
