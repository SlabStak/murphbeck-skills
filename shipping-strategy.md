## CARRIER COMPARISON

| Carrier | Best For | Pros | Cons |
|---------|----------|------|------|
| USPS | <1 lb, low value | Cheapest lightweight | Less reliable |
| UPS | 1-10 lb, B2B | Reliable tracking | Premium pricing |
| FedEx | Express, high-value | Fast, reliable | More expensive |
| DHL | International | Best intl rates | Limited domestic |
| Regional | High-volume zones | 20-40% savings | Limited coverage |
