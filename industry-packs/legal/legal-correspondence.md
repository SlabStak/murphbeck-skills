### Engagement Letter (New Client)

```markdown
[FIRM LETTERHEAD]

[Date]

VIA [EMAIL/CERTIFIED MAIL/HAND DELIVERY]

[Client Name]
[Client Address]
[City, State ZIP]

RE: Engagement Letter - [Matter Description]

Dear [Client Name]:

Thank you for selecting [Firm Name] to represent you in connection with the above-referenced matter. This letter confirms the terms of our engagement and outlines our mutual responsibilities.

## SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT

We have agreed to represent you in the following matter:

[Detailed description of the specific legal matter]

**This engagement is limited to the matter described above.** Any additional matters will require a separate engagement agreement.

## LEGAL FEES

Our fees will be calculated as follows:

☐ **Hourly Rate**
- Partner rate: $[XXX] per hour
- Associate rate: $[XXX] per hour
- Paralegal rate: $[XXX] per hour

☐ **Flat Fee**
- Total fee: $[XXX] for [scope]
- Payment schedule: [terms]

☐ **Contingency Fee**
- [XX]% of gross recovery
- Client responsible for costs regardless of outcome

## RETAINER

A retainer of $[AMOUNT] is required before we begin work. This retainer will be deposited in our client trust account and applied against fees and costs as incurred. You will receive monthly statements showing charges against the retainer. When the retainer is depleted, you will be asked to replenish it.

## COSTS AND EXPENSES

In addition to legal fees, you will be responsible for costs and expenses incurred in your matter, including but not limited to:

- Filing fees and court costs
- Service of process fees
- Deposition and transcript costs
- Expert witness fees
- Travel expenses
- Copying and postage
- Electronic research charges

## BILLING AND PAYMENT

You will receive monthly invoices detailing work performed and costs incurred. Payment is due within [30] days of the invoice date. Accounts more than [60] days past due may be subject to a [1.5]% monthly service charge.

## CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

To enable us to represent you effectively, you agree to:

1. Provide complete and accurate information
2. Keep us informed of any developments
3. Respond promptly to our requests for information
4. Attend scheduled meetings and court appearances
5. Pay invoices in a timely manner

## COMMUNICATION

[Attorney Name] will be your primary contact. We will communicate with you via [email/phone/mail] unless you request otherwise. We will keep you informed of significant developments in your matter.

## NO GUARANTEE OF OUTCOME

While we will use our best efforts on your behalf, we cannot and do not guarantee any particular outcome. The legal process involves many variables beyond our control.

## TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this engagement at any time upon written notice. If you terminate the engagement, you will be responsible for fees and costs incurred through the date of termination. If we terminate the engagement, we will provide reasonable notice and take steps to protect your interests.

## DOCUMENT RETENTION

At the conclusion of the matter, we will return your original documents. Our file will be retained for [7] years, after which it may be destroyed without further notice.

## AGREEMENT

Please indicate your acceptance of these terms by signing below and returning one copy along with the retainer payment. We look forward to working with you.

Sincerely,

[Attorney Name]
[Firm Name]

---

**ACKNOWLEDGED AND AGREED:**

___________________________ Date: _______________
[Client Name]
```

```markdown
[Additional relevant facts in chronological order]

[If statutory claim, include specific statute citation and relevant provisions]

1. [Specific demand - payment, action, cessation of conduct]

2. [Additional demands if applicable]

3. [Confirm compliance in writing]

## CONSEQUENCES OF NON-COMPLIANCE

- Filing a civil lawsuit seeking compensatory damages

This letter is not intended to be, and should not be construed as, a complete statement of the facts or law applicable to this matter. Our client expressly reserves all rights, claims, and remedies available at law or in equity.

Please direct all further communications regarding this matter to the undersigned.

Very truly yours,

[Attorney Name]
[State Bar No.]
[Firm Name]

cc: [Client Name] (via email)

*CONFIDENTIALITY NOTICE: This letter is intended only for the named recipient and may contain privileged or confidential information. If you are not the intended recipient, please notify the sender immediately and destroy this letter.*
```

### Cease and Desist Letter

```markdown
[FIRM LETTERHEAD]

[Date]

VIA CERTIFIED MAIL, RETURN RECEIPT REQUESTED

[Recipient Name/Company]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]

RE: CEASE AND DESIST DEMAND
    [Trademark/Copyright/Trade Secret/Other] Infringement
    Our Client: [Client Name]

Dear [Recipient]:

This law firm represents [Client Name] ("[Client]") in matters involving [the CLIENT TRADEMARK® trademark / CLIENT's copyrighted works / CLIENT's proprietary trade secrets]. We write concerning your unauthorized [use/reproduction/disclosure] of [specific protected material].

## OUR CLIENT'S RIGHTS

[Client] is the owner of [describe protected rights]:

☐ **Trademark Rights**
[Client] is the owner of [U.S. Trademark Registration No. XXX / common law trademark rights] in the [MARK NAME]® mark for [goods/services]. [Client] has used this mark continuously since [date].

☐ **Copyright Rights**
[Client] is the owner of [U.S. Copyright Registration No. XXX / valid copyright] in [describe work]. This work was first published on [date].

☐ **Trade Secret Rights**
[Client] is the owner of valuable trade secrets and confidential business information relating to [general description]. This information derives independent economic value from not being generally known.

## YOUR INFRINGING CONDUCT

It has come to our attention that you are [describe infringing conduct with specificity]:

- [Specific instance 1 with date and location/URL]
- [Specific instance 2]
- [Additional instances]

[If applicable: Screenshots/evidence of the infringing conduct are attached as Exhibit A.]

## LEGAL VIOLATIONS

Your conduct constitutes [select applicable]:

☐ **Trademark Infringement** under 15 U.S.C. § 1114 and/or § 1125(a)
☐ **Trademark Dilution** under 15 U.S.C. § 1125(c)
☐ **Copyright Infringement** under 17 U.S.C. § 501
☐ **Misappropriation of Trade Secrets** under [State] law and/or the Defend Trade Secrets Act, 18 U.S.C. § 1836
☐ **Unfair Competition** under [State] law
☐ **[Other statutory violations]**

## DEMAND

[Client] hereby demands that you immediately:

1. **CEASE AND DESIST** all use of [the infringing mark/copyrighted material/trade secrets] in any manner;

2. **REMOVE** all [infringing products from sale / infringing content from websites and social media / confidential information from your systems];

3. **DESTROY** all materials containing or embodying [the protected material];

4. **PROVIDE WRITTEN CONFIRMATION** of your compliance with demands 1-3 above;

5. **PROVIDE AN ACCOUNTING** of [all sales of infringing products / all revenue derived from infringing use / all persons to whom trade secrets were disclosed]; and

6. **PRESERVE ALL EVIDENCE** relating to your [use/disclosure] of [protected material], including but not limited to documents, communications, and electronically stored information.

## DEADLINE AND CONSEQUENCES

You must comply with all demands within **TEN (10) CALENDAR DAYS** of the date of this letter.

If you fail to comply, [Client] is prepared to pursue all available legal remedies without further notice, including:

- Seeking injunctive relief, including a temporary restraining order
- Filing suit for damages, including [statutory damages up to $150,000 per work infringed / Client's actual damages / your profits attributable to the infringement]
- Recovery of attorneys' fees and costs
- [Criminal referral for willful infringement, if applicable]

## PRESERVATION NOTICE

This letter constitutes formal notice to preserve all documents and electronically stored information relating to [the subject matter]. Failure to preserve such evidence may result in sanctions and adverse inference instructions.

## NO WAIVER

This letter is not intended to be a complete statement of [Client]'s rights or the facts and law applicable to this matter. [Client] expressly reserves all rights and remedies, whether at law or in equity.

Govern yourself accordingly.

Very truly yours,

[Attorney Name]
[Firm Name]

cc: [Client Name]

Enclosures: [Exhibit A - Evidence of Infringement]
```

## Quick Reference - Letter Types

| Letter Type | Purpose | Key Elements |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| Engagement | Establish representation | Scope, fees, retainer, responsibilities |
| Demand | Seek compliance/payment | Facts, legal basis, specific demand, deadline |
| Cease & Desist | Stop infringing conduct | Rights, violation, demand, consequences |
| Response | Reply to opposing demand | Denial/acceptance, defenses, counter-position |
| Settlement | Propose resolution | Terms, confidentiality, deadline |
| Court Cover | Transmit court filings | Documents list, fee, service info |
| Opinion | Advise client | Question, analysis, conclusion, limitations |
