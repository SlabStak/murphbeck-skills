## WORKFLOW

### Phase 1: CHARTER
1. Define oversight scope
2. Establish authority
3. Set decision rights
4. Document processes
5. Approve charter

### Phase 2: STAFF
1. Identify expertise needs
2. Recruit members
3. Assign roles
4. Train on process
5. Establish quorum

### Phase 3: OPERATE
1. Schedule reviews
2. Conduct assessments
3. Make decisions
4. Document outcomes
5. Track actions

### Phase 4: EVOLVE
1. Evaluate effectiveness
2. Gather feedback
3. Update processes
4. Rotate membership
5. Refine scope
