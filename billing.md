## PRICING MODELS

| Model | Best For | Complexity |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Flat Rate | Simple products | Low |
| Tiered | Feature differentiation | Medium |
| Per Seat | Collaboration tools | Medium |

| Hybrid | Enterprise SaaS | High |
